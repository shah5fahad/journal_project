Publication Policies

FAAI follows the guidelines of SCOPUS, NAAS & ICAR-NAAS for publishing high-quality scientific research in agricultural and animal sciences.

Journal Selection Criteria

Research papers must be submitted to a peer-reviewed journal following standard scientific guidelines.

Manuscript Structure

Manuscripts should follow the standard scientific format:

  • Title: Clear, concise, informative
  • Authors and Affiliations
  • Abstract (200â€“250 words)
  • Keywords (4â€“6)
  • Introduction
  • Materials and Methods
  • Results and Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References (APA, Harvard, or journal-specific)

Research Content Guidelines

  • Original research with clear objectives, methodology, analysis, and implications
  • Replicated field/lab studies are preferred
  • Statistical validation is required
  • Use recent literature (last 5â€“7 years)
  • Ethical standards must be followed for animal/human experiments

Data Presentation

  • Use clear tables, graphs, and figures
  • Units should be in SI format
  • Mention software used for analysis (e.g., SPSS, R, SAS)

References & Citation

  • Prefer recent (up to 10 years) and relevant references
  • Avoid excessive self-citations
  • Use proper citation tools (Mendeley, EndNote, Zotero)

NAAS Score Usage

When submitting for evaluation (e.g., promotion, grant), authors should highlight the NAAS score of the publication.

Ethical Compliance

  • Declaration of plagiarism check
  • Disclosure of conflicts of interest
  • Follow ICAR/University/Journal ethical norms

Submission Standards

  • Submit as per journal guidelines (word limit, file format, author template)
  • Include author contributions, funding sources, and acknowledgments

Publication Fee

A nominal Article Processing Charge (APC) of 10,000 INR, and for international authors 148 USD, may apply to support open-access dissemination.
