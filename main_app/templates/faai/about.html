About FAAI

Frontiers of Agri & Animal Innovation (FAAI) is a peer-reviewed, international journal dedicated to publishing high-quality, interdisciplinary research that addresses critical issues, pioneers innovative solutions, and explores emerging challenges in the fields of agriculture and animal sciences. Published by Curevita Research, FAAI adheres to NAAS and ICAR-NAAS guidelines, ensuring rigorous academic and ethical standards.

FAAI represents a progressive platform dedicated to advancing agriculture and animal sciences through innovation, technology, and sustainability. It reflects the frontier where traditional practices meet modern science, driving solutions for food security, environmental resilience, animal health, and sustainable farming systems.

The journal serves as a platform for bridging the gap between fundamental scientific research and practical applications, fostering knowledge exchange that supports holistic and sustainable advancements in global food systems. FAAI encourages contributions that integrate diverse perspectives, promote innovation, and drive positive change across agricultural and animal sectors worldwide.
