Our Journal

Curevita Innovation of BioData Intelligence (CIBDI) is an international, peer- reviewed, multidisciplinary journal dedicated to pioneering research at the intersection of Biology, Artificial Intelligence, and Data Science.

Curevita Innovation of BioData Intelligence signifies a pioneering platform that integrates biological sciences with advanced computational intelligence. It embodies the fusion of biosciences, big data analytics, and artificial intelligence to decode complex biological patterns, enhance healthcare decision-making, and accelerate discoveries in medicine, biotechnology, and environmental health.

Our mission is to accelerate biological innovation by integrating cutting-edge computational methods with advancements in bioinformatics, computational biology, systems biology, AI-driven healthcare solutions, biological data analytics, and machine learning applications in life sciences, systems biology, genomics, proteomics, biomedical engineering, and AI-driven healthcare solutions.

CIBDI provides a global platform for scientists, researchers, and innovators to publish original research, reviews, and technical advancements that transform biological data into actionable knowledge.
