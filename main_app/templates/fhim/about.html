About the Journal

Frontiers of Health Innovations and Medical Advances (FHIMA) is an international, peer-reviewed, multidisciplinary journal committed to publishing high-quality research, reviews, and case studies that explore transformative developments in healthcare, medical science, and allied health practices. FHIMA serves as a global platform for scientists, clinicians, policymakers, and innovators to share evidence-based knowledge that drives progress in health systems and patient care.

FHIMA represents the progressive boundary where cutting-edge research, breakthrough technologies, and transformative practices in health and medicine converge. The journal highlights pioneering solutions that improve human well-being â€” from preventive healthcare and diagnostics to therapeutic interventions, digital health, biotechnology, and integrative medicine.

The journal welcomes contributions from diverse domains including clinical medicine, public health, digital health technologies, biomedical research, nursing, physiotherapy, paramedical sciences, yoga and meditation, homeopathy, and Ayurveda. FHIMA particularly encourages interdisciplinary research that bridges modern scientific advancements with holistic and traditional approaches to health and wellness.

Mission Statement

The mission of FHIMA is to advance global health by publishing innovative, evidence-based research that integrates modern medical science with holistic and traditional healthcare practices. The journal aims to foster collaboration, inspire innovation, and promote sustainable healthcare solutions to address contemporary and future health challenges.

Vision Statement

To be a leading international journal that bridges the gap between cutting-edge medical science and holistic health traditions, fostering a healthier, more equitable world through innovation, integration, and knowledge dissemination.

Aims & Objectives

  • To provide a credible platform for publishing high-quality research in health and medical sciences.
  • To encourage interdisciplinary studies that unite modern healthcare and traditional healing systems.
  • To promote innovation in healthcare technologies, policies, and practices.
  • To support evidence-based approaches for improving patient care and community health.
  • To serve as a resource for healthcare professionals, researchers, and policymakers worldwide.

Our Purpose

With an emphasis on innovation, FHIMA promotes sustainable, culturally inclusive solutions that are adaptable to global healthcare challenges. Guided by rigorous peer review and ethical publication standards, the journal inspires collaboration, informs policy, and advances the frontiers of knowledge in health and medical sciences.
