About FERI

Frontiers in Environmental Revolutionary Innovation (FERI) is a peer-reviewed international journal devoted to advancing research and knowledge at the intersection of ecology, vitality sciences, environmental health, and sustainable innovation.

It provides an open platform for scholars, practitioners, policymakers, and innovators to share insights that build resilient ecosystems, healthy communities, and sustainable futures.

Quick Facts

  • Starting Year: 2025
  • Frequency of Publication: Quarterly
  • Format: Online
  • Subject Area: Environmental Health
  • Language: English

Meaning of the Title

Frontiers

Represents the cutting edge, the exploration of new knowledge, and the advancement of ideas in science, technology, and society.

Ecovitality

A unique concept that combines ecology and vitality. It signifies the strength, health, and dynamic life-force of ecosystems, highlighting the interconnectedness of environmental sustainability, biodiversity, and human well-being.

Resilient Innovation

Refers to creative, adaptive, and forward-looking solutions that not only address present challenges but are also strong enough to withstand future uncertainties, climate change impacts, and socio-economic pressures.
